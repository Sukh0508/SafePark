❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SafePark.

Is my phone number visible to people who scan my QR? +
Absolutely not. SafePark is built with privacy at its core. Your mobile number, email address, and home address are never exposed. The scanner only sees what you explicitly choose to share in your Privacy Settings.
How do I get my QR code on a sticker? +
After generating your QR code, you can download it as a high-resolution PDF. We also offer printed weatherproof stickers delivered to your doorstep for Pro and Society plan users.
What happens if someone scans my QR and wants to contact me? +
They can send you a message through SafePark's secure messaging system. You'll receive a push notification with their message and can choose to respond — all without revealing your personal contact details.
Can I register multiple vehicles? +
Yes! The Starter plan supports up to 2 vehicles. The Professional plan supports up to 5, and the Society plan has no limits. You get a unique QR code for each registered vehicle.
Is SafePark compatible with all vehicles? +
SafePark works with any vehicle — cars, motorcycles, trucks, electric vehicles, and more. As long as you have a registration number, you can register it on SafePark.
